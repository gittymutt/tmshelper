Instructions

Make Present button - When you are on class attendance page, click the 'Edit' button at the bottom of the screen, then click the ASA extension icon at top right of browser window. Then click 'Make Present'. This will make all students present. Change the few absent students to Absent, and you are done!

Campus and Assign To button - When you are on an ASA Tutoring Management page, click the dropdown boxes such as Class Participation, Required Reading, etc.. The Campus and Assign To dropdown boxes will become active. Now you can click the ASA extension icon at the top right of the browser window to automatically set the active Campus and Assign To dropdowns to 'Manhattan' and 'Learning Center', respectively.

Fill In button - Click the Fill In button to copy the text in the text box above the button to the selected boxes in the Tutoring Management page.
